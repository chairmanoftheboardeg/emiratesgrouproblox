Appointment of New Chief Executive Officer

Emirates Group Roblox Welcomes Daniel Thompson as Chief Executive Officer

Summary

The Emirates Group Roblox today announced the appointment of Daniel Thompson as its new Chief Executive Officer. This leadership transition represents a defining moment in the organization’s evolution, reinforcing its dedication to professionalism, unity, and sustainable growth under the framework of Vision 2026. As Co‑Founder and Chairman of the Board, Daniel Thompson now steps into the role of Chief Executive Officer to guide strategic direction and oversee executive leadership. His appointment underscores a renewed commitment to strengthening governance, revitalizing engagement across all divisions, and ensuring that Emirates Group Roblox continues to uphold the values upon which it was built. “This is not simply a change in leadership—it is a reaffirmation of our mission,” said Daniel Thompson, Chief Executive Officer, Emirates Group Roblox. “Together, we will restore vitality across our divisions, elevate operational standards, and position Emirates Group Roblox as a benchmark of excellence in the virtual aviation community.” -Ends- About the Emirates Group The Emirates Group is a global organization that includes Emirates airline, dnata, and a diverse portfolio of aviation, travel, and logistics businesses. With a presence in over 80 countries on six continents, the Group is committed to providing high-quality products and services, driven by a passion for excellence and a vision to connect the world.

Further details are communicated through official channels. This post exists to provide a stable public-facing record of direction and intent.
