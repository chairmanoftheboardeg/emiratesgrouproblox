Flight Guidelines & Rules

Check-In Protocol

Passengers must arrive at the designated terminal at least 15 minutes prior to the scheduled departure time. Late arrivals may be denied boarding to ensure on-time performance for the network.

Code of Conduct

Emirates Group Roblox enforces a strict roleplay standard. Trolling, disrupting staff operations, or ignoring Ground Crew/Cabin Crew directives will result in an immediate removal from the server.

Baggage & Seating

Economy passengers are subject to standard seating. First Class and Business Class bookings (where applicable via gamepasses or rank) receive priority boarding, lounge access, and premium cabin service.
