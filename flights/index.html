Official Advisory Bulletin

Flight Operations Standards

Ref: Legal, Safety & Compliance Framework (Sec. 10)

View Full Framework
10.1
Flight Deck Discipline

Pilots must follow published SOPs, briefings, and aircraft limitations at all times.

10.2
Clearance Compliance

Follow ATC clearances and OCC dispatch instructions strictly, unless safety requires an immediate deviation.

10.3
Runway and Taxi Safety

Unsafe taxiing, runway incursions, or reckless ground maneuvering is strictly prohibited.

10.4
Comms Protocols

Use correct channels and concise phraseology; avoid unnecessary chatter during active frequencies.

10.5
Professional Interactions

Maintain a professional tone with passengers, crew, ATC, and ground handling teams.

10.6
Controlled Emergency Roleplay

Emergency roleplay must remain controlled, authorized, and not be used to harass, derail, or disrupt operations.

10.7
Aircraft Handling Integrity

Intentional stalling, crashing, or executing unrealistic maneuvers in official sessions is prohibited.

10.8
Crew Coordination

Flight crew must coordinate effectively with cabin leadership (Pursers) when roleplay requires interaction.

10.9
Operational Reporting

Personnel must comply with all logging, checklists, and post-flight reporting requirements where currently in effect.
