Loyalty & Rewards

Elevate Your
Journey.

In the Emirates Group Roblox network, economy travel is completely free. We use tier points to reward our most dedicated flyers, unlocking premium features, priority access, and VIP treatment.

Progress Through the Tiers

As you fly with us and participate in the corporate network, you accumulate Skywards Miles and Tier Miles. Elevate your status across our distinct membership tiers to unlock everything from priority boarding to unlimited Business Class luxury.

Blue Tier

Standard Membership

Blue Tier is automatically granted to all members upon registering an Emirates account on our platform. This serves as the foundation of your loyalty journey.

  • Full access to book standard Economy flights across the network.
  • Participate in the overarching Emirates Group experience.
  • Begin earning Skywards Points and Tier Miles as you progress.

Silver Tier

Priority Travel

Silver Tier marks your entry into priority travel and enhanced recognition. Members experience a more refined journey with early access to premium opportunities.

  • Priority Silver Boarding Call, granting earlier boarding access.
  • 2 complimentary Business Class flights per month.
  • Ability to select one flight of your choice per month (route/aircraft).
  • Official Silver Role on Discord & Exclusive Website Profile Badge.
Division III

Gold Tier

Gold Tier represents a significant step into premium travel, combining efficiency, luxury, and priority recognition across nearly all touchpoints within the Emirates Group.

Enhanced Privileges (Includes Silver Benefits)

  • 10 complimentary Business Class flights per month.
  • 1 complimentary First Class flight per month.
  • Access to a Personal Airport Butler service.
  • Immediate response to all inquiries with priority support.
  • Official Gold Role on Discord & Distinguished Gold Profile Badge.
Division IV • Ultimate Elite

Platinum Tier

The highest level of Emirates Skywards membership, reserved strictly for our most dedicated and elite members. Platinum flyers experience the Emirates journey without limitation—defined by exclusivity and prestige.

The Ultimate Privileges (Includes Gold Benefits)

  • Unlimited Business Class flights per month.
  • 3 complimentary First Class flights per month.
  • Private Chauffeur Drive service to and from the airport.
  • VIP Boarding via private stairs.
  • Exclusive Platinum Role on Discord & Premium Platinum Profile Badge.
