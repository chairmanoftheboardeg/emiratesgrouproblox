Luxury All The Way

Welcome to our most
exclusive travel experience.

Whether you're travelling with colleagues or family, enjoy our exceptional service as we fly you privately around the world. Designed for your comfort.

Personalised Fine Dining

Our lounge converts into your personal restaurant in the sky. Whether you'd like a formal sit-down meal, banquet-style buffet, simple cocktails and canapés or to enjoy light snacks as you sit back to watch the latest blockbuster movie.

You can choose when you want to dine and have a menu tailored to your personal taste. Only the finest, freshest locally sourced ingredients are used by master chefs, served at your convenience.

Rejuvenating Shower Spa

Refresh mid-flight in the luxury Shower Spa or separate powder room. Finished in classic walnut and marble, take some time to slow down the pace and indulge yourself.

A temperature-controlled shower with luxury bathrobes, fine linens, and exclusive organic spa products provide a welcome opportunity to unwind in style. Arrive refreshed and ready to take on the day.

Ultimate Comfort

Travel the world
your way.

Explore all the possibilities the world has to offer with the flexibility of private jet travel. Choose when you’d like to fly and select your departure and destination airports within or outside the Emirates network.

From Private Suites that let you retreat into your own world to a lounge that can be rearranged to your needs, Emirates Executive is a fully personalised experience.

Your Own Private Suite

Sliding privacy doors let you retreat into your own world, with a fully flat-bed seat, memory foam mattress, feather down duvets, ambient lighting, and a personal mini-bar.

Cabin Configuration

We can arrange the cabin to suit your travel requirements. The aircraft carries up to 19 guests in the seated configuration and sleeps up to 15.

Dedicated Cabin Crew

Our specialist, multilingual Emirates Executive cabin crew offer you the highest level of our personalised service. Your comfort is our top priority.

Award-Winning Entertainment

Relax and enjoy up to 3,000 channels of on-demand entertainment on our award-winning system, ice. Watch from your 32-inch screen in every Private Suite.

Your Office In The Sky

can't wait.

Arrange your flight to meet the needs of your busy schedule. Convert your living area into your own board room. The cabin is equipped with the latest technology and a conference table can be arranged for your business meetings at 40,000ft.
