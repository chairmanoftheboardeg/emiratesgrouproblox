Luxury All The Way

What to expect

From custom dining menus to rejuvenating spas and high-tech business suites, your private flight is entirely bespoke.

Personalised Fine Dining

Our lounge converts into your personal restaurant in the sky. Whether you'd like a formal sit-down meal, banquet-style buffet, simple cocktails and canapés or to enjoy light snacks as you sit back to watch the latest blockbuster movie.

Rejuvenating Shower Spa

Refresh mid-flight in the luxury Shower Spa. A temperature-controlled shower with luxury bathrobes, towels, and exclusive organic spa products provide a welcome opportunity to unwind in style.

Retreat into your own world

Slide the privacy doors closed to your own Private Suite. Sit back or stretch out in sumptuous surroundings. Up to 15 guests can sleep on the aircraft in converted full-sized beds, featuring memory foam mattresses and feather down duvets.

Explore new worlds

Relax and enjoy up to 3,000 channels of on-demand entertainment on our award-winning system, ice. Watch from your personal 32-inch screen in every Private Suite.

When business can't wait

Convert your living area into your own board room. The cabin is equipped with Wi-Fi, video conferencing, and a conference table that can be arranged for your business meetings at 40,000ft.

Breeze through the airport

Experience a smooth journey on your departure and arrival. Save valuable time with discreet boarding, private terminal access, and chauffeur-drive vehicle transfers.
