Corporate Overview

Who We Are

Our Story

dnata is the lifeblood of the tarmac. At Emirates Group Roblox, our ground crews ensure that every flight departs precisely on time, every cargo pallet is strictly secured, and every aircraft is fully serviced before the first passenger even steps aboard.

Our Leadership

Operations are orchestrated by a dedicated team of Ground Operations Directors. These leaders coordinate complex tarmac movements in real-time, acting as the critical link between Air Traffic Control and the flight deck.

Ground Operations

Our Services

From the moment the wheels touch the ground to the final pushback, dnata personnel handle every logistical aspect of the aircraft's turnaround.

Ground Handling & Towing

Precision is paramount. Our ramp agents operate heavy tugs and pushback tractors to safely maneuver wide-body aircraft out of active gates and across complex taxiways.

Flight Catering Services

Delivering First Class culinary experiences. Specialized dnata catering trucks dock seamlessly with the aircraft fuselage, loading high-quality meals and supplies required for long-haul routes.

Aviation Refueling

Maintaining the lifeblood of the fleet. Our logistics teams manage high-capacity fuel bowsers to quickly and safely refuel aircraft during rapid turnaround windows.
