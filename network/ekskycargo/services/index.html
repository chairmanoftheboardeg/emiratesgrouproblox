SkyCargo
Operational Logistics

Vital Payloads.

SkyCargo is not just about flying—it is about executing precise roleplay scenarios. Discover the crucial logistics our pilots execute daily within the PTFS airspace.

The Lifeblood of the Network

In the Emirates Group Roblox network, our hotels, resorts, and corporate hubs do not supply themselves. SkyCargo pilots are responsible for fulfilling simulated supply chains, ensuring the broader roleplay ecosystem thrives.

Every cargo flight is a mission. Under the watchful eyes of Corporate Training Officers, rookie and senior pilots alike must handle their payloads with precision, navigating the PTFS skies securely.

Heavy Machinery & Industrial

Hauling massive, oversized roleplay assets required for corporate construction projects and heavy industry operations within the PTFS map.

Primary Fleet: B747F, A380

Luxury Automotive Transport

Secure transport of high-value VIP vehicles, executive fleet cars, and ground assets required by Emirates Holidays and luxury resorts.

Primary Fleet: B777F

Medical & Humanitarian

High-stakes, time-sensitive roleplay scenarios. Pilots must swiftly deliver medical supplies and humanitarian aid across the PTFS airspace during crisis events.

Primary Fleet: A350-900

E-Commerce & Express Courier

Fast-paced, multi-stop routes delivering general commercial goods and express mail. Tests a pilot's efficiency and ability to maintain strict timetables.

Primary Fleet: A350-900

Aircraft Parts Relocation

Critical corporate logistics. Transporting engines, fuselage components, and maintenance gear to stranded aircraft or active repair hangars.

Primary Fleet: B747F

Pilot Evaluation Runs

The most important service we offer. Dispatch runs actively monitored by Training Officers to evaluate a rookie's readiness for commercial passenger flights.

The Dispatch Protocol

SkyCargo operations are not free-flight sessions. They are highly coordinated logistics missions evaluated by Emirates Group Corporate.

1. Mission Assignment

Pilots receive a dedicated manifest detailing the cargo type, weight, and strictly required flight path within PTFS.

2. Execution & Monitoring

Training Officers monitor altitude, heading, ATC communications, and landing precision throughout the duration of the haul.

3. Debrief & Graduation

Successful execution of heavy lift missions qualifies rookie pilots to graduate into the Emirates Airlines commercial passenger division.
