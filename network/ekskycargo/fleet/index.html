Fleet Logistics

The Heavy Lifters.

Explore the dedicated PTFS fleet. These are the aircraft that forge our newest aviators and manage the high-capacity logistics of the Emirates Group network.

Primary Logistics Hauler

Boeing 777F

The backbone of our PTFS cargo operations. The 777 Freighter provides the perfect balance of range and payload capacity, making it the standard proving ground for pilots advancing into wide-body operations.

PTFS Operational Specs

Training Level Intermediate
Primary Role Heavy Logistics
Route Profile Global / Long-Haul
High-Capacity Transporter

Boeing 747F

The iconic "Queen of the Skies" operates exclusively for massive-scale freight. Its signature nose-loading door allows for oversized roleplay cargo, testing a pilot's precision in heavy load handling.

PTFS Operational Specs

Training Level Advanced
Primary Role Oversized Freight
Route Profile Intercontinental
Modern Fleet Addition

Airbus A350-900

A state-of-the-art addition to the cargo rotation. The A350 is highly efficient and is utilized by Training Officers to evaluate a pilot's proficiency with modern, computerized flight systems.

PTFS Operational Specs

Training Level Intermediate
Primary Role Express Cargo
Route Profile Ultra Long-Haul
Superjumbo Transport

Airbus A380-800

While traditionally a passenger airliner, SkyCargo utilizes modified A380 airframes for extreme logistics scenarios. Piloting this behemoth is the final crucible for senior trainees before graduation.
