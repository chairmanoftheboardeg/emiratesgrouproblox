PTFS Operations Division

The Ultimate
Proving Ground.

In the Emirates Group Roblox network, SkyCargo serves a critical purpose. Operating exclusively in Pilot Training Flight Simulator (PTFS), we manage the heavy logistics and roleplay cargo required to keep the global network functioning.

Crucially, SkyCargo guarantees continuous, engaging operations for our team. Even during periods when the community might be less active and passenger amounts on commercial flights are low, our staff and pilots can still have an amazing time operating these heavy-lift cargo flights. It ensures the skies are never empty and our crew always has a vital mission to execute.

Our Services

Vital Logistics

Every cargo flight is a mission. Under the watchful eyes of Corporate Training Officers, rookie and senior pilots alike must execute specific roleplay scenarios to supply the network.

Heavy Machinery

Hauling massive, oversized roleplay assets required for corporate construction projects and heavy industry operations within the PTFS map.

Luxury Automotive

Secure transport of high-value VIP vehicles, executive fleet cars, and ground assets required by Emirates Holidays resorts.

Medical & Relief

High-stakes, time-sensitive roleplay scenarios. Pilots must swiftly deliver medical supplies and humanitarian aid during crisis events.

Express Courier

Fast-paced, multi-stop routes delivering general commercial goods. Tests a pilot's efficiency and ability to maintain strict timetables.

Parts Relocation

Critical corporate logistics. Transporting engines, fuselage components, and maintenance gear to stranded aircraft or active repair hangars.

Pilot Evaluation Runs

Dispatch runs actively monitored by Training Officers to evaluate a rookie's readiness for commercial passenger flights.

Primary Haulers

Boeing 747 & 777F

The absolute backbone of our PTFS cargo operations. Providing the perfect balance of range and payload capacity, these aircraft are the standard proving ground for pilots advancing into wide-body operations.

  • ✔ Intermediate to Advanced Training Level

Modern Fleet

Airbus A350-900

A state-of-the-art addition to the cargo rotation. The A350 is highly efficient and is utilized by Training Officers to evaluate a pilot's proficiency with modern, computerized flight systems.

  • ✔ Express Courier & Medical Logistics
Superjumbo Transport

Utilized exclusively for massive-scale logistics roleplay. Piloting this behemoth is the ultimate test of handling and precision, acting as the final crucible for senior trainees before graduation.
