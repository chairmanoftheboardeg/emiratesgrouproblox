PTFS Operations Division

The Ultimate
Proving Ground.

In the Emirates Group Roblox network, SkyCargo serves a critical dual purpose. Operating exclusively in Pilot Training Flight Simulator (PTFS), we manage the heavy logistics and roleplay cargo required to keep the global network functioning.

More importantly, SkyCargo is the crucible for our new pilots. Before touching a passenger flight, rookies are assigned to cargo routes. Under the strict supervision of our Training Officers, pilots must prove their navigation, handling, and adherence to protocol.
