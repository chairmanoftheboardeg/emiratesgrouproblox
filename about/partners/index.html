Unity Aviation Alliance

Emirates Group Roblox is a proud member of the Unity Aviation Alliance, a prestigious network founded by neoptic. This alliance unites elite virtual airlines on the Roblox platform, fostering collaboration, shared operational standards, and seamless codeshare opportunities. Together, we work to elevate the aviation experience for pilots and passengers alike.

Austrian Airlines

Owned by Alexander, Austrian Airlines is a trusted partner renowned for its precision and Alpine expertise. They proudly operate the specialized Innsbruck to Dresden route using modern Airbus A320 aircraft, mastering the challenging mountainous terrain with ease. Blending Austrian hospitality with German elegance, they offer passengers a scenic and professional connection.

Malaysia Airlines

Chaired by Yometaru, Malaysia Airlines Berhad stands as the virtual national flag carrier of Malaysia. As a key strategic partner, they provide a gateway to Southeast Asia, delivering the best in comfort and authentic hospitality. Whether you are an aviation enthusiast or a passenger, Malaysia Airlines guarantees a seamless and relaxing journey with a focus on premium service standards.
