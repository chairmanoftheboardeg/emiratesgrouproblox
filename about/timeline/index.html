Our Heritage

Corporate Timeline

The chronological history of our relentless pursuit of perfection in the virtual skies, from our founding to our current global dominance.

December 2023

The Foundation

Emirates Group Roblox is officially founded by Tyler Nicholas and Kareem Malhas. The core architectural vision is established to bring unprecedented realism, luxury, and professional hierarchy to the Roblox aviation community.

January 2024

Structural Formation

The Board of Directors drafts the inaugural Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs). Early infrastructure, including Discord communication channels and preliminary fleet assignments, are finalized in preparation for launch.

February 2024

Inaugural PTFS Operations

A historic milestone as the first commercial operations officially begin within PTFS (Pilot Training Flight Simulator). The initial flights set a completely new standard for roleplay professionalism and strict ATC compliance.

April 2024

Operational Consistency

The organization successfully completes its first 20 scheduled flights. This proves the viability of the EGR scheduling matrix, verifying that ground services and flight ops can perform flawlessly under live passenger pressure.

August 2024

Establishment of EGRAU

To maintain our rapid growth without sacrificing quality, the Emirates Group Roblox Aviation University (EGRAU) is conceptualized, creating a rigorous internal training pathway for all future staff.

Late 2024

Unprecedented Growth

By the close of 2024, EGR crosses the massive threshold of flying 500 total passengers. This serves as a definitive testament to the community's trust in our brand and the unyielding dedication of our staff.

December 2024

Industry Recognition

The virtual aviation community formally recognizes our dominance. EGR is simultaneously nominated for "Best Airline Of The Year" in PTFS and awarded the "Best Airline Of The Month" by AASA.

January 2025

The Next Frontier: ROAV

Transitioning beyond third-party simulators, EGR begins the highly classified development of ROAV (Personal Game Development). This marks our evolution from an airline into a bespoke, standalone aviation technology developer. We also secure back-to-back nominations for Best Airline of the Month.

Mid 2025

Subsidiary Expansion

Laying the groundwork for regional dominance, the Executive Board begins incubating distinct subsidiaries, eventually leading to the successful launch and integration of brands like Fly Dubai Roblox and SkyCargo.

November 2025

A Strategic Corporate Alliance

In a monumental corporate shift, Emirates Group Roblox officially joins the Tyler Nicholas Group portfolio. This integration unlocks massive strategic backing, unifies our global network, and positions EGR as the undisputed crown jewel of virtual corporate aviation.

Present Day

Global Dominance

With a state-of-the-art centralized web infrastructure, over 1000+ active members, and an ever-expanding fleet, the journey of Emirates Group Roblox continues. The horizon is just the beginning.
