About Us

Our Story

Founded by Tyler Nicholas and co-founded by Kareem Malhas, the organisation has grown into a multi-divisional ecosystem built around professionalism, realism, and operational integrity.

Why EGR exists

We built EGR to set a higher standard for virtual aviation. The goal is a corporate-grade operating model where every division has a defined mandate and where execution is consistent across operations.

Our approach prioritises structure, clear responsibilities, and disciplined coordination, supported by governance and safety culture.

Professional culture

Culture is defined by behaviour. We expect respectful communication, consistency in execution, and compliance with standards. This is how we protect the environment and maintain quality over time.

Independence disclaimer

Emirates Group Roblox is an independent virtual aviation organisation. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or a subsidiary of the real-world Emirates Airline or The Emirates Group (dnata).
