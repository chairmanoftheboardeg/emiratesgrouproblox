Core Technologies

Live Telemetry API

Our web architecture is fully synced to a custom Supabase relational database. Live flights, career applications, newsroom updates, and staff moderation are updated across all subdomains in real-time without requiring a page refresh.

Hyper-Realistic Fleet

We utilize highly detailed mesh modeling for our Airbus A350 and Boeing 777 fleets. Every aircraft features working avionics, custom-scripted control surfaces, and meticulously designed interior cabins mapped to accurate seating charts.

Platform Integration

Powered by Falcon Ltd, our Discord communications server is hard-linked to Roblox endpoints. Promotions, automated flight announcements, and ban appeals are executed seamlessly between the two platforms.

System Spotlight

The Operations
Control Center.

Our O.C.C. (Operations Control Center) is the digital brain of Emirates Group Roblox. It replaces the chaotic, standard roleplay structure found in other groups with a highly disciplined, tool-driven environment.

  • Automated Dispatch: Generating precise flight plans, route data, and weather simulations for our pilots.
  • Centralized ATC: Proprietary radar screens allowing Air Traffic Controllers to manage airspace safely and effectively.
  • Ground Sync: Instant communication relays between the flight deck and dnata ground handling agents.
